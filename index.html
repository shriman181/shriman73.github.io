The Platform

One system for your
entire mobility stack

Eloriva connects document collection, form automation, compliance monitoring, and case tracking into a single workflow your team will actually use.

Passport & Document OCR

Upload a passport, visa, or civil document and Eloriva extracts every field automatically — name, nationality, expiry, MRZ codes — and populates your immigration forms instantly. No manual re-entry.

Document Verification

AI-powered authenticity checks cross-reference document data with issuing authority formats, catch inconsistencies, flag expiring credentials, and ensure completeness before submission — not after rejection.

Auto-Population Engine

Forms for H-1B, Blue Card, Skilled Worker, Employment Pass, and 300+ other visa types are auto-filled from your verified document data. One source of truth, zero duplicate entry.

Case Status Tracking

Every employee's immigration journey in one place. Real-time status updates from government portals, deadline alerts, document expiry notifications, and a full audit trail for every action taken.

Policy Change Monitoring
across 200+ jurisdictions

Immigration law changes every day. Eloriva monitors regulations across 200+ countries and territories in real time — the moment a rule changes, your affected cases are automatically flagged and your team is notified with a plain-language summary of what changed and what to do.

TB

Tejas Bhovad

Founder & CEO

Tejas built Eloriva after watching multinational HR teams — smart, capable people — spend half their working week managing immigration via email threads and shared spreadsheets. He saw firsthand how a single missed policy update or misplaced document could derail an employee's relocation, a team's timeline, and a company's trust.

Before founding Eloriva, Tejas worked at the intersection of enterprise HR tech and compliance, where he spent years mapping the exact failure modes in global mobility operations. He noticed that the tools built for immigration law firms were too rigid for in-house HR teams — and the generic HR platforms were simply not built for the complexity of multi-jurisdiction visa management.

"The hardest part of corporate immigration isn't the law — it's the logistics. Documents get lost in email. Policy changes are discovered after a rejection. I built Eloriva to make the operational layer invisible, so HR teams can focus on the humans they're moving."

Eloriva is Tejas's commitment that no company should ever lose a hire — or a week — to broken paperwork workflows.
